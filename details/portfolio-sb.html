Project Information

  • Category: Data Analysis
  • Project Date: Jun 2022 - Dec 2022

Background

A research project for a client organization in order to develop business intelligence in one of their process.

Highlights

  • Collaborated with two other members in the team to come up with a solution proposal for their process.

  • Developed visualizations using Microsoft PowerBI to gain further insights to the client's data.

  • In this project, I learned how easy it was to create an application using Microsoft PowerApps. And is it especially beneficial for small organizations, who can't afford to build a fully customized application. For simple functions like data entering, displaying, and a simple managed workflow, Microsoft PowerApps is a cheap alternative to having an application for a business where the data storage can be a database, excel file, or Micrsoft Lists.

Technologies Used

Microsoft PowerApps, Microsoft PowerBI
